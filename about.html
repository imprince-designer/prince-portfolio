About

I design products that earn trust.

Six years in. Three countries. Fintech, consumer, AI. I've learned that the best design decisions come from deeply understanding the person on the other side of the screen — not just the brief.

Photo coming soon

I'm Prince — a Senior Product Designer based in Gurugram, India. I specialise in end-to-end product design for fintech and consumer products, working across the full stack from early research to final pixel.

My career has taken me from building ChefKart's design practice from the ground up — scaling the team from 1 to 10 and the product to 100K+ daily users — to designing AI-native financial products at BharatPe and Syfe across India, Singapore and Australia.

I'm drawn to products where design directly impacts financial access and behaviour. At BharatPe I designed for merchants who had never used a bank app. At Syfe I made wealth management feel approachable for a new generation of investors. These aren't just UX problems — they're trust problems. And trust is built through clarity, consistency and care.

Outside work, I'm a photographer. Two years behind the lens taught me to notice what most people walk past — a skill that quietly shapes every design decision I make.
