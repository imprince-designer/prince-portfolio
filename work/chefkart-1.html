Overview

When your product pivots, your homepage needs to pivot with it

ChefKart connects customers with professional home cooks. In 2022 the company pivoted from subscription-first to on-demand services. But by 2024 the homepage still spoke the language of subscriptions. New users landed on a page designed for a product that no longer existed — causing confusion, drop-off and cancellations.

Introduction

My journey in the design team

In 2022, ChefKart pivoted to introduce new on-demand services but the existing app wasn't built to support these offerings. By 2024, users were struggling to understand the services, leading to confusion, drop-offs, and last-minute cancellations. I worked on design solutions to clarify services and streamline the booking process, using user feedback and research to guide the improvements.
